MAD-SARU

AGARTHA / Good Vibes

2005年北陸を拠点にDJ活動を開始し、大小様々なpartyでplayしており、現在、地元福井では国内のトランスアーティストをゲストに迎えたサイケデリックトランスパーティAGARTHA(アガルタ)を定期的に開催中。また、毎年夏に開催しているOpen Air Party''Rising Sun''のオーガナイザーGood Vibesの一員としても活動中。時間帯にあったPlayスタイルを意識しロングミックスとショートミックスを自在に操るミックスワークでフロアーをまとめ上げる。テクノ～トランスといった独自の選曲で日々試行錯誤を繰り返しDJ修行中...奮闘中!!!!

He started DJing activities based on Hokuriku in 2005, and played at various parties, large and small, and is currently holding a psychedelic trance party AGARTHA (Agarta), with local trance artists as guests at local Fukui. He is also a member of Good Vibes, the organizer of the Open Air Party 'Rising Sun', which is held every summer. Consciously play style that was in the time zone, put together the floor with mix work to manipulate long mix and short mix freely. A trial and error is repeated every day by original selection such as techno-trance DJ training.

blackout

京都を拠点に活動するビートメイカー/DJ。 関西某所にてオープンエアパーティーblackoutをオーガナイズする。 パンク、ハードコア、テクノ、クラシックピアノなど、バックボーンは多岐に渡り日々貪欲に音を求め続ける。様々なシーンをtranceに昇華し新しいヴィジョンを作るのが目標。

till death we do art.

Japanese beatmaker in Kyoto. He has organized a outdoor music party "blackout". He has a great variety of musical experience. In childhood, he used to learned classical piano and played drums at a punk band when young. By the early 2000s, he was discovering techno and began DJ. His goal is to create a new sound vision of trance by mixing other genres.

Alan Ritchie

NHA TRANG BEATS ELECTRIC

A vinyl junkie since the late 80’s and a regular DJ on the London club circuit since the early 90’s & illegal warehouse parties and raves all over Europe. Founder of London based radio station Frequency Radio, DJ & party promoter in London, Vietnam & Cambodia for 25 years with my roots firmly set in Acid House, Uk Hardcore/Rave, Chicago House, Detroit Techno & Electro.


Booking manager and resident DJ for Pontoon Pulse in Phnom Penh Cambodia for 4 years & still booking top acts like Mr.C, "Evil" Eddie Richards, Marco Lenzi, Moodymanc, DJ Woody, Dicky Trisco, Pete Herbert, Alex Ferrer, Cuban Brothers, Anna Hanna & many more. I now live back in Vietnam Dj'ing at various bars & clubs and booking Dj's for Pontoon Pulse, Phnom Penh, Cambodia where i still regularly DJ.

Jessie Belters

Cardiff/Swansea

UK based Jessie Belters has warmed up for DJ royalty, huge names including Omar-S, DJ Stingray and Stojche, to name a few. She has played in various festivals, clubs and parties worldwide including an all vinyl set in legendary Mecca of techno Tresor Berlin, Dimensions Festival in Croatia and more recently tore it up at the world renowned jungle rave located in a remote corner of Cambodia, Kerfuffle. Jessie Belters runs the decks in style throwing down solely underground sounds. From heavy techno, house and electro.

Walts

After emerging from the underground music scene of Bristol, UK, Walts has become a household name in the notorious music scene in Hakuba, Japan. Co- founder of the ever Popular night, The Deep Edition, an event focussed on bringing the UK sound to Japan as well as showcasing big name DJs and local heroes in the scene. Walts has a unique, highly eclectic sound, mixing through cross genres spanning through Electro, House, Breakbeat, Minimal and Techno, tailoring every set to be deep and rhythmic, keeping high energy across the dance floor.
